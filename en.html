Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

Koala Baby collects the following data:

  • Account data: email address, name (optional)
  • Child data: name, date of birth, gender
  • Diary entries: feedings, sleep, diapers, weight, notes, trips, milestones
  • Shopping list: product names, categories

2. Data Storage

Data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) on servers in the European Union. Access to data requires logging into the user's account.

3. Photos

Photos (child avatar, milestones, trips) are stored only locally on the user's device. They are not uploaded to any server or shared with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties, advertising companies, or business partners.

Exception: the account sharing feature — users can voluntarily share their diary with another caregiver (partner) using an invitation code.

5. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which screens are most visited). This data does not allow identification of specific users.

6. User Rights

  • Data export: ability to download data in PDF or CSV format
  • Account deletion: permanent deletion of all data from the server (Profile → Delete account)
  • Logout: at any time

7. Security

Data is protected by Firebase security mechanisms (encryption in transit and at rest, access rules). Only the account owner and persons invited through the sharing feature have access to user data.

8. Children

The app is intended for parents/caregivers. We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 16 years of age. Child data (name, date of birth) is entered by the parent/caregiver.

9. Policy Changes

We will notify users of significant changes to the privacy policy through an in-app notification.
